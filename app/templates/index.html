Mission

Kolab aims to be the worlds best and only collaboration platform for the next generation, to inspire a future of technology innovation.

Why is it important?

The UK is facing a crisis; we have a technical and engineering skill shortage. There are not enough programmers to fulfil demand. As our society becomes more automated, we must foresee the future demand and ensure the next generation has the right skills for a technology driven future.

The internet is not built for children. The majority of child friendly sites have host games and are generally intended to amuse the attention span of a child. However, we lack websites suitable for inspiring and educating children. We must build websites to enable the next generation to learn and share their knowledge. We must allow them to interact with their work with the sense of accomplishment and share with one another. Work should not sit dormant on a computer desktop. It should be exhibited and shared.

The Solution

Kolab aims to handle this problem through a single collaborative platform. It allows kids to easily upload technical artifacts (code) and homework files. They are then able interact easily using the browser and work in teams whilst all being online. Key to Kolab is safety and we aim to create a safe and welcoming community.
